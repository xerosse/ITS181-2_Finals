Welcome to Dog City

Dog City is your local veterinary clinic for animal health, welfare, and public safety. We are dedicated to ensuring a safe and healthy community for both our citizens and our animal residents. Our programs focus on essential public health services like free anti-rabies vaccinations, responsible pet ownership, and population control. We are your partner in promoting animal welfare across the city.

Dogs Available for Adoption

Why Adopt

Save a Life

Adopting directly rescues an animal from a shelter, freeing up space for another dog in need of temporary housing and care.

Gain a friend

Many shelter dogs are already house-trained and past the destructive puppy stage, offering you a clearer picture of their personality and temperament.

Great Value

Adoption fees often include initial veterinary care like spaying/neutering, vaccinations, and microchipping, which saves you hundreds of dollars.
